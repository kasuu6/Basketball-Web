Speed Dribbling

3 sets 45 sec

Instructions:

  • Start in an athletic stance with feet shoulder-width apart
  • Begin with the ball in your right hand
  • Pass the ball behind your back to your left hand
  • Bring the ball around the front of your body back to the right hand
  • Maintain a continuous circular motion around your body
  • Keep your core tight and knees slightly bent throughout

Figure 8 Dribble

3 sets 60 sec

Instructions:

  • Get into an athletic stance with your knees bent and your feet shoulder-width apart.
  • Hold the ball in your dominant hand
  • Dribble the ball around your dominant leg, keeping it low and controlled.
  • Pass the ball between your legs to your opposite hand
